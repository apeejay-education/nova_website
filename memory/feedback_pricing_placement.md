Don't show pricing on every page or in stats rows. It belongs only on the /pricing page and the very bottom CTA / Nova Lounge section of solution pages.

**Why:** Dilutes the premium positioning and clutters mid-page content that should be focused on outcomes and features.

**How to apply:** When building stats rows, feature sections, or hero sub-copy on solution pages, never include the ₹35 figure. Reserve it for the dedicated pricing page and the final CTA card at the bottom of each page.
