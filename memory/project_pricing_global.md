Nova pricing display: show $1/student/month (global benchmark) with a visual strikethrough or "was" treatment, then ₹35/student/month as the launch/India offer. Communicates global-grade product at India pricing.

**Why:** Signals they are building for global schools, not just Indian market. The USD anchor makes ₹35 feel like an exceptional deal.

**How to apply:** Pricing page and any pricing mentions should lead with the $1 USD benchmark, then the ₹35 launch price. Use "Introductory Launch Pricing" framing.

Curriculum coverage:
- Currently supported: CBSE, ICSE
- Coming soon: IB, Cambridge (IGCSE), and other international curricula
- Long-term: building for global schools

**How to apply:** When building Solutions/segment pages, CBSE and ICSE are the only live ones. IB, Cambridge, IGCSE should be "Coming Soon" — same treatment as Tell Nova.
